About Us

Meet our team

Welcome to the Ireland of your dreams, we hope yours too

If you love hiking, you are fascinated by wonderful views, looking for adventure and you would like to know more about the interesting history of Ireland, then you have come to the right place

We are a small group of friends who are passionate about hiking in Ireland. We have walked the Irish mountains for many kilometers and each time it is an amazing and unforgettable experience for us.

Our certified guide Arek and the main organizer who is passionate about the history of Ireland, can tell you many interesting stories about this beautiful, scenical and wonderful country.

We hope to see you soon on one of our trips
